Whiley v0.3.2 Released!

Thursday, December 16th, 2010
Well, another release was long overdue!!! This one includes lots and lots of bug fixes, particularly to the type system, and support for dictionaries (aka maps) is now in place. Also, the language has been split in two parts: the base language (flow-sensitive + structural typing), and the extended langauge (which includes constraint checking).
